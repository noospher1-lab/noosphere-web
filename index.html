The Core Problem

Governance ignores quality of thought

01

Traditional Democracy

One person, one vote — regardless of expertise. The nuclear physicist and the person who can't spell "nuclear" carry equal weight on energy policy.

02

Token Governance

Crypto DAOs give power to wallets, not minds. 1% of holders control most votes. Plutocracy doesn't become democracy by moving on-chain.

03

The Missing Layer

No existing system measures argument quality. Noosphere does. PoI Score — five dimensions of reasoning, evaluated by AI, validated by peers, recorded forever.
